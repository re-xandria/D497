Dining and Grocery Stores

Taniti offers a delicious mix of culinary experiences, combining traditional island flavors with international cuisine. Visitors can enjoy fresh-caught fish and fragrant rice dishes at local eateries, hearty American-style meals, or the vibrant tastes of Pan-Asian cooking. For those who prefer cooking their own meals, the island’s grocery stores are well-stocked with fresh produce, pantry staples, and unique local ingredient.

The Coconut Grill

Specializing in freshly grilled seafood paired with fragrant island rice, this beachfront eatery offers unbeatable ocean views. Try their famous pineapple-glazed mahi-mahi.

Seabreeze Bistro

A cozy spot serving local fish dishes with a modern twist, like tamarind-marinated tuna. Their outdoor patio is perfect for sunset dining.

Harbor View Diner

This family-friendly diner serves up classic American comfort food, including burgers, fries, and milkshakes. Don’t miss their homemade pie.

Island Catch Kitchen

Known for its catch-of-the-day specials and coconut rice, this casual spot celebrates the freshest flavors of Taniti.

Sunset Eats

Offering a mix of local and international dishes, this restaurant is best known for its crispy fried snapper and refreshing tropical smoothies.

Palm's American Grill

Craving a taste of home? This casual eatery serves hearty American fare, including BBQ ribs, mac and cheese, and craft beers.

Taniti Table

A chic dining experience offering Pan-Asian cuisine with island flair. Popular dishes include teriyaki-glazed salmon and coconut curry noodles.

Ocean's Bounty Café

A small eatery specializing in grilled swordfish, prawn skewers, and traditional island rice pilaf. Their house-made hot sauce is a must-try.

Island Wok

This Pan-Asian gem serves up sizzling stir-fries, dim sum, and fresh sushi. Locals rave about their spicy mango chicken.

Bayfront BBQ Shack

A laid-back spot serving up smoky barbecue dishes with an island twist, like jerk chicken wings and pineapple-glazed pork ribs.

Tropical Treasures Supermarket

A one-stop shop for fresh produce, seafood, and imported goods. They offer an impressive selection of local jams and spices.

Taniti Fresh Market

Known for its organic and locally sourced ingredients, this market is perfect for picking up tropical fruits and artisanal breads.

Harbor Groceries

A smaller store stocked with vacation essentials, from snacks to sunscreen. Their fresh deli sandwiches are great for picnics.

Island Essentials Mart

Convenient and affordable, this store offers everyday groceries and a small selection of souvenirs.

Coral Cove Convenience Store

Open 24/7, this shop is perfect for late-night snack runs or grabbing last-minute supplies. Their local pastries are a hidden gem.

Seaside Farmer’s Market

This weekly market is the best place to find fresh fruits, vegetables, and handmade crafts. Vendors often share recipes for their local specialties.
